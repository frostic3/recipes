Ingredients:
 - 2 cup boiling water
 - Secret Ingredient
 - 1 pack of noodles

Directions:
 1. To the boiling water, add the Secret Ingredient and mix them uniformly.

 2. Now, add the noodles pack to the boiling water.

 3. Keep stirring until all water has either been absorbed or evaporated.

 4. Serve hot. Enjoy your noodles :3
